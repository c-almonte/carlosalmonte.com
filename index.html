My Story

Believer. Artist.
Father. Strategist.

Three words have followed me across every chapter of my life: Innovation. Improvisation. Invention. I didn't choose them -- they chose me.

I came up as an artist. Music was the first language. Jazz taught me to listen before I spoke, to trust the space between notes, and to find structure inside chaos. I spent a decade playing in New York City before the digital world pulled me in.

What followed was 28 years across global agencies like R/GA and Publicis Sapient, the startup ecosystem at Techstars, and eventually founding Motevo Co in 2021. Different stages, same instinct -- show up, read the room, build something that matters.
